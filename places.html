Dawki is most famous among tourists for the "fascinating Umngot River, also known as Dawki River, which features water so clear it could be mistaken for a see-through glass.”

-There is no specific time to visit Dawki town. On the contrary, you can visit this place just about any time of the year.However, the winter season is considered a good time to visit here..

- Dawki Bridge, is a suspension bridge over the Umngot River. It was constructed in 1932 by the British.
